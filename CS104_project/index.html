One-Player

  1. Hey, there! Let's learn how to play this game
  2. The field you will play on is of grid type which will have the dimension which you like it to have :-)
  3. You will get 3 life-lines, which means 3 chances to stay alive in the game. As soon as you lose all the lifelines and open one more fielder block, I will have to bid sayonara to you.
  4. There are 11 fielders in the team (which you chose) scattered randomly on the field, each cell
    containing atmost 1 and atleast 0 fielders
  5. You will have to pick any of the cell based on your choice, if there is no fielder in that cell then
    hey! you scored 1 run, and if you find any fielder in there then you will lose one friend
  6. There are some surprise cells which if you choose, can score 4 or 6 runs in one go or just
    get bombed and DIE, based on your luck
  7. Here we are done with instructions. Wish you all the best!
